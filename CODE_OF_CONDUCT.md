# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to a harassment-free, welcoming community for everyone,
regardless of background, identity, experience, or viewpoint.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, constructive, and considerate.
- Focus criticism on ideas and code, not people.
- Welcome good-faith questions and different perspectives.
- Respect privacy, consent, and project boundaries.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discriminatory language, threats, personal attacks, doxxing,
sexualized or unwanted attention, and deliberately disruptive conduct are not
acceptable.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the project maintainers through the
GitHub organization contact method. Maintainers may remove content, issue a
warning, restrict participation, or take other proportionate action. Reports
will be handled as confidentially as practical.
